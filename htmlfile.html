Latest Research

Blockchain for Large Language Models Security and Safety: A Holistic Survey

Published in SIGKDD Explorations, December 2024.

This survey explores the intersection of blockchain technology and large language models, highlighting potential use cases, challenges, and future directions. My focus was how blockchain technology can address critical security vulnerabilities in large language models (LLMs) like data poisoning, prompt injections, and unauthorized data exposure. I created the systematic categorization (BC4LLMs taxonomy) that also shows related works in this area which offers novel frameworks and definitions to advance the field. By highlighting potential challenges and research directions, I aim to inspire targeted innovations in blockchain-integrated LLM security.
