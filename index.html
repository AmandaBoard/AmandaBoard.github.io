Blockchain for Large Language Model Security and Safety: A Holistic Survey

This published paper was co-authored by myself and my peer, we discuss the multitude of ways in which we can improve the safety and security of LLMs using Blockchain technologies. I focused on the intersection of blockchain and LLM security, where current blockchain solutions can aide against LLM security threats like prompt injection, data leakage, and general adversarial attacks.

Published in the ACM Digital Library, 2024.

Check it out here! dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3715073.3715075
